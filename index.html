THE FRAMEWORK

Why Most AI Implementations Fail

Companies skip straight to building (Phase 3) without understanding what to build or why. That's expensive trial and error. With AI nowadays, it's never been easier to implement the right solution wrong, and the wrong solution right, and fail to get the ROI you expected.

My 3-Phase Methodology ensures you build the right solution in the right order for maximum ROI.

1

Inside-Out: Find the Friction

I interview your team to discover where time disappears. Not where you think the problems are—where they actually are.

What happens:

  • 1-on-1 interviews with team members
  • Shadow key workflows for a day
  • Map where hours are bleeding
  • Identify manual bottlenecks
Outcome: A clear map of where your operation is inefficient
2

Outside-In: Prioritize by Value

Not all friction costs the same. I trace your customer journey backward from payment to find what blocks revenue or burns cash.

What happens:

  • Map your revenue journey
  • Calculate cost of each friction point
  • Rank opportunities by ROI
  • Identify quick wins vs. long-term plays
Outcome: A prioritized roadmap of what to automate first
3

Middle-Out: Implement at Leverage Points

Instead of automating everything, I find the single leverage point where fixing one thing fixes five things downstream.

What happens:

  • Build automation at the leverage point
  • Create ripple benefits across workflows
  • Train your team on the new system
  • Measure actual time/cost savings
Outcome: One automation that solves multiple problems

The Result?

Instead of scattering your resources across 10 small automations, you get one strategic implementation that delivers measurable ROI in weeks, not months.
